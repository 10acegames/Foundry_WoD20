The Experience Item is used in two ways:
You add it to an actor to give them experience - type {{localize "wod.types.expspent"}}
You add it if they spend experience - type {{localize "wod.types.expgained"}}.

The text in {{localize "wod.labels.description"}} is the text what the Experience Item will display on the sheet itself.
